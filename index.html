Our Core Service

Calculation of Value

When you need a credible, standards-compliant number. Fast.

A Calculation of Value is a professionally certified engagement where we agree on specific approaches and procedures upfront, then apply them to arrive at a supportable value. The result is a written report — NACVA-compliant, delivered in English — documenting what the business is worth and how we got there.

Appropriate for buy-sell agreements, succession planning, partner transitions, estate and gift transfers, and internal decision-making. Delivered in 2–3 weeks.
