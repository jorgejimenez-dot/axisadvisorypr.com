Calculation of Value

A clear answer to the question: what is my business worth?

A professionally certified calculation of value — the practical tool business owners and their advisors need for the decisions that don't require a formal opinion of value, but still demand rigor and credibility.

Ideal for planning, negotiations, and internal decisions where you need a credible number backed by professional standards. Not intended for litigation, regulatory filings, or financing where a formal opinion is required. For those situations, we can refer you to a trusted colleague offering comprehensive valuation services.

Common Use Cases

  • Buy-sell agreements
  • Succession planning
  • Internal decision-making
  • Early-stage negotiations
  • Periodic / recurring valuations
  • Tax planning support (consult your tax advisor for filing requirements)

What You'll Receive

A professional report (typically 8–20 pages) that clearly states what your business is worth and explains how we arrived at that number — including what comparable businesses have sold for, what your earnings look like after adjustments, and what factors affect your company's specific risk profile. We walk you through the findings on a call so you understand every page.

What's Included

  • Valuation approaches appropriate to the subject company (typically market-based with income approach support where warranted)
  • Normalized earnings with documented adjustments
  • Company-specific risk assessment
  • Professional report with methodology, assumptions, and calculated value indication
  • Analyst walkthrough of findings
