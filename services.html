Three levels of service. One standard of quality.

Every engagement is governed by NACVA professional standards.

CalcReport

A professional calculation of value for internal decisions, planning, and negotiations.

The CalcReport delivers a defensible, standards-compliant calculation of value — the practical tool business owners and their advisors need for the decisions that don't require a full opinion of value, but still demand rigor.

Ideal for planning, negotiations, and internal decisions where you need a credible, defensible number — without a six-figure engagement. Not intended for litigation, regulatory filings, or financing where a formal opinion is required. For those situations, see Full Valuation.

Common Use Cases

  • Buy-sell agreements
  • Succession planning
  • Internal decision-making
  • Early-stage negotiations
  • Periodic / recurring valuations
  • Estate & gift tax planning

What's Included

  • Multi-method analysis (income, market, and/or asset-based as appropriate)
  • Normalized earnings with documented adjustments
  • Company-specific risk assessment
  • Professional report with methodology, assumptions, and conclusion
  • Analyst walkthrough of findings
Turnaround 2–3 weeks typical
Pricing $1,500 – $2,500 fixed fee
Delivery English or Spanish

Full Valuation

When the situation demands a fully developed opinion of value.

A valuation engagement that considers all three approaches to value — income, market, and asset-based — and produces a formal conclusion of value. Designed for situations where the analysis will face external scrutiny.

If your valuation will be reviewed by a court, a lender, or a regulatory body — or if the transaction stakes are high enough that you need the most thorough analysis available — this is the right scope.

Common Use Cases

  • Litigation support
  • Regulatory / Hacienda compliance
  • Financing applications
  • SBA / bank lending
  • Complex ownership structures
  • Act 60 transactions
Turnaround 4–6 weeks typical
Pricing Starting at $4,000
Scope All three valuation approaches
A full valuation produces a conclusion of value — a formal opinion suitable for external use including litigation, regulatory proceedings, and financing. Engagements follow NACVA Professional Standards and are tailored to the specific purpose and intended use.

Annual Update

Keep your valuation current without starting from scratch.

A streamlined refresh of a prior CalcReport using updated financials. Designed for buy-sell agreements, recurring compliance, and businesses that need an annual fair market value determination.

If you already have a CalcReport from AXIS and need it updated with the latest year's numbers — same methodology, same rigor, faster turnaround, lower cost.

Common Use Cases

  • Annual buy-sell agreement updates
  • Recurring estate & gift tax compliance
  • Year-over-year value tracking
  • Partner equity reconciliation

What's Included

  • Updated financial analysis with current-year data
  • Revised market multiples and risk assessment
  • Comparison to prior-year conclusion
  • Professional update report with methodology notes
Turnaround 1–2 weeks typical
Pricing $750 – $1,250 fixed fee
Prerequisite Prior AXIS CalcReport
Annual updates use the same methodology and standards as the original CalcReport. Available to clients with a prior AXIS engagement completed within the last 24 months. For businesses requiring a new baseline analysis, see CalcReport.
