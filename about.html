Standards-driven analysis. Real-world perspective.

AXIS Independent Advisory is a credentialed business valuation practice serving Puerto Rico's closely-held businesses.

Our work is grounded in professional standards and shaped by years of hands-on experience across public accounting advisory, corporate finance, and capital markets operations.

That range of experience — from valuation and due diligence engagements to managing real-time treasury flows and executive financial reporting — gives our practice a practical edge that pure-accounting firms often lack.

We don't just model businesses. We understand how they run.

AXIS was founded to fill a gap we saw firsthand — small businesses in Puerto Rico deserve the same quality of valuation analysis that larger markets take for granted.

Our Approach

Independence First

We have no financial interest in the outcome of any valuation. No brokerage commissions. No advisory fees tied to transaction value. Our only product is an objective, defensible number.

Built for the Local Market

We understand Puerto Rico's regulatory landscape, tax incentive structures, lending norms, and the dynamics of family-owned and closely-held operations. Our models are calibrated for the businesses that actually operate here.

Standards Without Excess

Every engagement follows NACVA Professional Standards, consistent with SSVS-1 and USPAP. But standards compliance doesn't require a 200-page report when a focused CalcReport will serve the purpose. We match the scope to the need.

Embedded, Not Competing

For CPA firms and advisors, we operate as an extension of your practice — not a competitor for your client relationships. Your clients stay yours. We provide the specialized analysis behind the scenes.
